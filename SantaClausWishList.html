Santa Claus's Wish List

Rules: You are in a mall, in front of Santa Claus. He is asking what do you want for Christmas. He is also telling you that this year you were a kind person and he allows you to have 5 wishes. In the box below, Santa Claus tells you to enter your wishes (enter at least 5 wishes). Be carefull! If you enter more than 5 wishes, Santa Claus will think that you are a selfish person and it will not be guarenteed that he will add your wishes in his list!

Enter your wish below!

If you want to be sure that your wish is on Santa's list, you can search it on the box below.
