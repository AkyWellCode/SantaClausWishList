Santa Claus's Wish List

Rules: You are in a mall, in front of Santa Claus. He is asking what do you want for Christmas. He is also telling you that this year you were a kind person and he allows you to have 3 wishes. In the box below, Santa Claus tells you to enter your wishes (enter at least 3 wishes). Be carefull! If you enter more than 3 wishes, Santa Claus will think that you are a selfish person and it will not be guarenteed that he will add your wishes in his list!

Enter your wish below!

After 3 days you want to check if Santa's elves added your wish in their database. Just enter your wish in the box below and see if the elves did their job.
